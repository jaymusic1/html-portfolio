The Best Movies According to Jay Jenner

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lord of The Rings Trilogy

Simply put the best movie adaptation of a book. It became a standard in the industry regarding to direction, film script and acting.

The Shutter Island

Another partnership between the director Martin Scorsese and the actor Leonardo DiCaprio. The plot is mindbreaking and both acting and direction are outstanding.

Star Wars Original and Prequel Trilogies

Another masterpiece, this one by George Lucas. These films are major successes of the entire cinema history.
